The Mr. Dork 3 Development Timeline

Trace the whimsical journey of Mr. Dork, from his charming beginnings to thrilling adventures.
Here are our milestones, subject to change.

2007 • The Birth of Mr. Dork

Mr. Dork was born out of pure passion for platformers, as a small Flash game that quickly captured the hearts of players. It was the beginning of a journey full of charm and quirky adventures.

2009 • Mr. Dork 2 Released

Following the success of the first game, Mr. Dork returned with even more challenging levels and more humor. The game solidified its place in the hearts of classic platformer lovers.

2023 • Mr. Dork 3 Project Begins

After years of love and anticipation, the development of Mr. Dork 3 began. Reviving the spirit of classic platformers while adding modern twists, Mr. Dork is ready to embark on his biggest adventure yet!

October 2024 • Early Access Phase 1 Begins

The Early Access journey of Mr. Dork 3 begins! In Phase 1, players will experience the core mechanics, explore early levels, and help shape the game with their feedback.

Phase 2 • More Levels & Features

Phase 2 will introduce new worlds, more challenging levels, and enhanced gameplay mechanics. Expect new surprises and quirky elements that make Mr. Dork 3 even more fun!

Phase 3 • Couch Co-Op & Battle Mode

In Phase 3, Couch Co-Op and Battle Mode will be introduced, allowing friends to join the fun and compete against each other in time-based challenges!

Phase 4 • Global Leaderboards & Online Multiplayer

Phase 4 will introduce Global Leaderboards and Online Multiplayer via Steam Party. Compete with players around the world and show your mastery of Mr. Dork's world!

Phase 5 • Community Feedback Integration

We will actively gather and integrate player feedback, refining gameplay mechanics and adding features requested by the community, making this game truly a collaborative effort.

Phase 6 • Final Alpha Testing

In this final phase before launch, we’ll focus on rigorous testing to ensure a polished experience. Expect bug fixes and final adjustments based on player testing.

Full Release • Coming Soon

Once Early Access is complete, Mr. Dork 3 will officially launch with full features on multiple platforms. Stay tuned for the final release and get ready for the complete adventure!
