Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to our Privacy Policy. It details how we handle your personal information when you visit mrdork3.com, play our game Mr. Dork 3: One Last Doodle!, participate in our alpha testing, pledge support for the game, or engage with us in any other form.

We, MayStudios, located in Burscheid, Germany (also referred to as “MayStudios” or “us”) are responsible for the control and processing of your data.

Personal data refers to any information that can be used to identify an individual. The type of data we gather and process depends on our relationship with you and the context of our interaction.

For any queries regarding your personal data processing, feel free to contact us via email at contact@maystudios.net.

2. Personal data we collect

The following sections provide a comprehensive description of the personal data we gather:

  • Data about our Players
  • Data about Pledges
  • Data about Alpha Registrants
  • Data on Bug Reports
  • Data on Website Visitors

Data about our Players

When you play Mr. Dork 3 or participate in our alpha testing, we collect specific data to provide and improve our services. This may include your gameplay data, system specifications, interactions within the game, feedback, and any reported issues.

Data about Pledges

When you pledge support for the game, we collect necessary information to facilitate and track your contribution. This information can include your full name, contact information, payment details, and the details of your pledge. We use this data to process your pledge, provide updates, and facilitate any rewards tied to your support.

Data about Alpha Registrants

When you register for our alpha testing, we collect data such as your name, email, and possibly hardware specifications to evaluate your eligibility and facilitate your participation. We use this data to communicate with you about the alpha testing process, your role, and any updates related to it.

Data on Bug Reports

As a player, when you submit bug reports through our in-game system, we gather information related to the bug, your account, and your system settings. This data helps us understand the issues better, fix them promptly, and improve the overall gaming experience.

Data on Website Visitors

When you visit mrdork3.com, we collect data on your interactions and usage of our website. This can include information such as your IP address, browser type, browsing history, and any information you voluntarily provide us, like your email for newsletters.

3. Recipients of personal data

We do not share or sell your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

4. Cookies

Our website utilizes cookies, storing specific information about how you use our website.

  • Functional Cookies: Enhance the website's performance by providing improved functionality and personalization based on your preferences and settings.
  • Statistical Cookies: Register visits and traffic sources to aid in measuring and improving the website's efficiency.
  • Session Cookies: Are removed from your computer once you close your internet browser.

5. Data Retention Period

We retain your personal data for as long as your player account remains active. If you choose to close your account permanently, we will erase your personal data accordingly.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have a number of rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You have the right to correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your personal data when there’s no compelling reason for us to keep using it.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You have the right to block or suppress the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to obtain and reuse your personal data across different services.
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@maystudios.net.
