Privacy and Personal Data Protection

This policy informs you about how we collect, use, and store the personal information that you provide to us through this site. It applies only to the site www.maystudios.net and does not apply to personal information collected by any other means.
We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time without notice, and we invite you to refer to it regularly.

Our Commitments

We are committed to respecting data protection laws. No personal information will be collected without your knowledge, will be used for purposes other than those of this site, and will not be transferred to third parties, except the professionals listed on the site, without your authorization or without you being clearly informed.
The personal information about you may be retained and used for a period that does not exceed the duration necessary for the purposes of their collection. We are committed to taking all necessary precautions to preserve the confidentiality and integrity of this data and prevent any communication to unauthorized third parties.

Collection of Personal Data

You can visit most parts of our site without providing any personal information. In certain cases (forms, sending an email, etc.), we may ask you for personal information, such as your name, email address, etc. No personal information is collected without your consent. These details are requested only to the extent that they are necessary.

Use and Retention of Personal Data

The only personal information we may request on our site are:
- your civil status (title, name)
- your email address (personal or professional)
This information is used only for the purposes for which it was requested. They are not used for any other purpose.

Security and Confidentiality of Personal Data

We consider your personal information confidential. We strive to take reasonable technical measures to prevent any loss or misuse during the collection or storage of the data you have provided and to prevent any communication to unauthorized third parties.

Transfer of Personal Data

Your personal information is intended for MayStudios. Under no circumstances will it be communicated to third parties.

Transfer of Personal Data Abroad

We do not carry out transfers of data abroad.

Anonymous Data

We may also use cookies, and possibly, other captures of environmental variables. In this case, you are informed of the existence of these processes, their purpose, and the ability to oppose them.

Photographic Credits

The photographs on the site may not be reused without the authorization of MayStudios.
