FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mr. Dork 3?
Mr. Dork 3 is a classic action-platformer that brings back the charm of the original games while adding modern gameplay mechanics. In this adventure, you’ll guide Mr. Dork through perilous worlds, solve tricky puzzles, and tackle action-packed levels on his most exciting journey yet.
How long does it take to complete the game?
The playtime of Mr. Dork 3 depends on how much time you spend exploring levels and finding secrets. On average, it takes about 8 to 10 hours to complete the main game, but discovering all the secrets and mastering the bonus levels can significantly extend that time.
What platforms are supported?
Mr. Dork 3 will first be available on Steam, Google Play, and iOS. After that, we’ll release it on Steam Deck, Xbox, PlayStation 4, and PlayStation 5. Finally, we plan to launch it on Nintendo platforms. Stay tuned for release dates on each platform!
Is there a multiplayer mode?
Yes and no! We are currently working on a couch co-op mode and a battle mode where players race against the clock. In the future, we plan to add global leaderboards and online multiplayer via Steam Party. This will allow you to play co-op and competitive modes with friends or other players online. Stay tuned for updates!
Will there be DLCs or expansions?
Yes, we plan to release some free updates with new levels and challenges to expand the gameplay experience. We are also open to releasing DLCs that include new worlds and story content based on player feedback.
What are the minimum system requirements for the PC version?

Minimum Requirements:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel i5-4590 / AMD Ryzen 3 1200
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 960 / AMD Radeon R9 290
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 10 GB available space

Recommended Requirements:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel i7-7700K / AMD Ryzen 5 1600
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 1060 / AMD Radeon RX 580
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 10 GB available space
Are there hidden levels or secrets in the game?
Yes! Mr. Dork 3 is packed with hidden levels, secrets, and collectibles. Players who keep a keen eye out and thoroughly explore each level will be rewarded with bonus content and special challenges.
How often will the game be updated?
We plan regular updates, especially during the Early Access phase, to fix bugs and add new content based on player feedback. After the official release, we will continue to provide periodic updates with new features and improvements.
Will there be Steam Achievements?
Yes, Mr. Dork 3 will feature a variety of Steam Achievements. These range from simple milestones to more difficult challenges for hardcore players to unlock.
How big is your studio? Do you have big ambitions or is this a passion project?
Mr. Dork 3 is a labor of love created by two close friends from two small indie studios: MayStudios and Simsalabim Studios (part of EpicNova Production). We’ve been working together for years, driven by our shared passion for making games that bring joy to players. While Mr. Dork 3 is born out of our love for the platformer genre, we also dream big and aim to create more exciting projects in the future. This game represents not only our creativity but also our dedication to delivering a fun, memorable experience for all who play it.
